Arts Undergraduate Programs

Undergraduate degrees in Arts provide a foundation in humanities, social sciences, media, and creative fields.

  • Bachelor of Social Work (BSW) - Community Service, Social Welfare
  • Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM) - Hospitality, Tourism
  • Bachelor of Library Science (B.Lib.Sc) - Library & Information Management
  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) - Teaching, Educational Training
